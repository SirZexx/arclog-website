Data Management

Delete Your Data

How to clear your library and remove tracking data from Arclog without deleting your account.

1

Clear Your Entire Library

Arclog includes a "Clear All Data" option that permanently removes your entire library and tracking progress from our servers. Your account, profile, and comments remain intact.

1
Open the Arclog app and log in to your account.
2
Go to your Profile tab and tap the Settings icon (gear icon in the top right).
3
Scroll to the Data section.
4
Tap Clear All Data. A confirmation dialog will ask if you are sure.
5
Confirm by tapping Clear All. A second and final warning will appear. Tap Delete Everything to proceed.
2

What Gets Removed

Clearing your data removes the following from our servers:

Permanently Deleted
  • All titles in your library (anime, manga, movies, TV shows, books)
  • All watch and read progress (episode counts, chapter counts)
  • All status tags (watching, completed, planning, dropped, on hold)
  • All ratings you have given to titles
3

What Stays

Clearing your library does not affect the following. These remain on your account:

Not Affected
  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your profile information (username, display name, bio, avatar)
  • Your comments and discussion posts
  • Your likes on other users' content

If you want to remove everything including your profile and comments, use the Delete Account option instead.

4

Important Things to Know

This action is irreversible. Once your library is cleared, there is no way to recover your titles, progress, or ratings. You would need to re-add everything manually.

Deletion happens immediately. Your library data is removed from our database right away with no delay or waiting period.

You can also remove individual titles. If you only want to remove specific titles rather than your whole library, you can do so from the title's detail page by tapping the remove option. This is not an all-or-nothing action.
